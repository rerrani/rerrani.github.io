Raphaël Errani

Hi! I'm a postdoc at the University of Victoria, interested in the clustering properties of dark matter on small scales, and particularly in the tidal evolution of dwarf galaxies. My work lies at the interface between dynamical modelling, numerical simulations and observational constraints. Previously, I did a PhD at the University of Edinburgh (2019), and graduated from the University of Padova (2015). Click here for a brief CV.
