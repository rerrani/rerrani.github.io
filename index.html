Raphaël Errani

Hi! I'm a postdoc at Carnegie Mellon University, interested in the clustering properties of dark matter on small scales, and in particular in the tidal evolution of dwarf galaxies. My work lies at the interface between dynamical modelling, numerical simulations and observational constraints. Before moving to Pittsburgh, I was a postdoc at the Strasbourg Observatory, worked as a CITA fellow in Victoria, and did a PhD in Edinburgh.
